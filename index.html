Welcome to Project Verdansk

Our goal is to demonstrate that with the help of small, concealable, affordable, Do-It-Yourself devices such as Arduino and Raspberry, you can exerce your right to free speech wherever you go. So far, Project Trinity's website will be hosted by an Atmel ATMega328P. However, we are looking forward to add USB support for endless possibilities.

Why Verdansk ? Well, because Verdansk is an unstable, at war zone; ideal conditions to demonstrate our project if it were to be real.

We will use a manifesto as placeholder here. We stand in no way behind what will be written below. We name it "The Three Axis of Evil, or of greater good ?". It is a reference to 43rd President of the United States George W. Bush's speech on January 29, 2002. The manifesto constitutes itself of 3 axis, available below.
