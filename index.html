Welcome to Project Trinity

Our goal is to demonstrate that with the help of small, concealable, affordable, Do-It-Yourself devices such as Arduino and Raspberry, you can exerce your right to free speech wherever you go. So far, Project Trinity's website will be hosted by an Atmel ATMega328P. However, we are looking forward to add USB support for endless possibilities.
